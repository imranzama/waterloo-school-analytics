EQAO Dataset & Methodologies

This analytics project combines educational statistics from public and Catholic schools across Waterloo Region. Key data layers include:

  • EQAO Standard Results: Sourced from the Education Quality and Accountability Office (eqao.com) for Grades 3, 6, 9 and OSSLT, outlining percentage of students meeting or exceeding provincial standard (Levels 3 & 4).
  • Ontario School Information System (OnSIS): Supplies student school-level enrollment counts and board boundaries.
  • WRDSB Open Data Catalog: Provides spatial location data, panel listings, and metadata.
  • Statistics Canada: Integrates local family income percentiles to help contextualize achievements.

Data Disclaimers

EQAO scores represent a single, focused indicator of school academic benchmarks. Educational researchers suggest evaluating EQAO results in combination with local course success metrics, student demographics, extracurricular opportunities, and specific literacy and mathematics multi-year action plans.
